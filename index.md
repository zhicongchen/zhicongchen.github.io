## About

I'm a Ph.D. student in Communication at [Web Mining Lab](http://weblab.com.cityu.edu.hk/) and [Department of Media and Communication](https://www.cityu.edu.hk/com), City University of Hong Kong, supervised by [Dr. Xiaofan Liu](https://www.cityu.edu.hk/com/Profile.aspx?u=xliu347) and co-supervised by [Prof. Jonathan Zhu](https://www.cityu.edu.hk/com/Profile.aspx?u=enjhzhu). Before that, I received my Master in Journalism and Communication from Nanjing University in 2018 and Bachelor of Engineering in Software Engineering from Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2016.

I was deeply attracted by computational social science as an undergraduate in 2015, since when I started to study social sciences. My on-going PhD project examines the diffusion, use, and social impacts of anonymous communication technology (e.g. [The Tor network](https://torproject.org/) and [The Dark Web](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dark_web)) through quantitative and computational approaches.
