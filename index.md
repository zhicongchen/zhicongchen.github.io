# Bio

Zhicong is a second-year Ph.D. student in Communication at Web Mining Lab and the Department of Media and Communication, City University of Hong Kong, under training for interdisciplinary research in computational social science by Dr. Xiaofan Liu and Prof. Jonathan Zhu. His current research examines the use and social impacts of privacy-enhancing and anonymous communication technology (e.g. Darknet) by analyzing online user behavior using computational methods (e.g. network analysis and text mining).
