# Bio

Right now I'm a first-year PhD student at Department of Media and Communication, City University of Hong Kong, with affiliation to [Web Mining Lab](http://weblab.com.cityu.edu.hk/blog/). I have received by MA in Journalism and Communication from [Computational Communication Collaboratory](https://computational-communication.com/collaboratory/), Nanjing University, and BE in Software Engineering from Nangjing University of Posts and Telecommunications. My research interests are generally related to scientific research about communication, media technology and society, under the paradigm of computational social science. In the past three years, I have been working with large-scale dataset collected from human digital footprints, including both online (mobile reading) or offline (call record data through mobile phone) in order to reveal interesting patterns in human communication behavior.
